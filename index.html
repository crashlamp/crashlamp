Welome to crashlamp.

If you are looking for a studio to record your music and or video at a very reasonable price, crashlamp is here for you. Crashlamp is owned and operated by Shane Landstrom at his home in Rock Island, Illinois. Crashlamp has a wide selection of high quality microphones, preamps, eq's, compressors and effects. The studio will be opening when it is deemed safe and stay at home orders due to Covid-19 are lifted
